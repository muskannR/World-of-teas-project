Our Mission

Perception of tea in Ireland saddens us a lot. People are well-versed in coffee, alcohol, but the knowledge of tea is limited. Tea is a world of its own that is locked for most.
We aim to promote tea culture in Ireland.

Our mission is to guide curious minds into the world of Tea. Our aim is to help others to learn about its magical world and the art of Living Slowly. Through Tea we are able to create space for others to slow down, savour the moment and see the magic in the ordinary.
